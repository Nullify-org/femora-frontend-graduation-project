# Product Details Page

## Overview
Detailed product information with purchase options.

## Functionality

- Display product information
- Product image gallery
- Product specifications
- Seller information
- Customer reviews
- Add to cart/wishlist
- Price information

## Product Information Section

- Product images/gallery with zoom
- Product name and rating
- Price and availability
- Quantity selector
- Add to cart button
- Save to wishlist
- Share product

## Product Details

- Description
- Specifications/features
- Dimensions/weight
- Material/condition
- Warranty/return policy

## Seller Information

- Seller name
- Seller rating
- Seller reviews count
- Seller response time
- Contact seller button

## Customer Reviews

- Average rating and count
- Review list with:
  - Reviewer name
  - Rating
  - Review text
  - Review date
  - Helpful count
- Filter/sort reviews
- Write review button (for purchasers)

## Related Products

- Suggested similar products
- Frequently bought together
- Customers also viewed

## Interactions

- Select quantity
- Add to cart
- Save to wishlist
- View seller profile
- Contact seller
- Share product
- Write review
